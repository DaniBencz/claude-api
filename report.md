# Q1 Sales Report

## Summary

Sales grew 12% quarter over quarter, driven by strong performance in the
Northeast region and the launch of two new product lines.

## Regional Breakdown

The Northeast region led growth with a 20% increase, followed by the
West region at 9%. The South region was roughly flat versus last quarter.

## Product Performance

The new "Aria" and "Bolt" product lines together accounted for 15% of
total revenue in their first full quarter on the market.

## Outlook

Leadership expects continued growth next quarter, with a cautious eye on
supply chain costs heading into Q2.
